Postgraduate Programme

M. Tech in Computer Science& Engineering and Computer Network Engineering

Appa Institute of Engineering and Technology is offering the Visvesavaraya Technological University approved 2 year PG course for the students who have a B.E degree in Computer Science / Information Science and Engineering/ Electronics and Commutations Engineering / Telecommunication Engineering/ Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The course is offered by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering by highly qualified and experienced Teaching Faculty. Intake to these course is 18.The Computer Science & Engineering department has set up advanced laboratory facilities and the students are trained in these laboratories regularly by allotting suitable time slots in the regular time table.

M. Tech in Digital Electronics and VLSI & Embedded System

Appa Institute of Engineering and Technology is offering the Visvesavaraya Technological University approved 2 year PG course for the students who have a B.E degree in Electronics and Commutations Engineering/TelecommunicationEngineering/Instrumentation Technology. The course is offered by the Department of Electronics & Communications Engineering by highly qualified and experienced Teaching Faculty. Intake to this course is 18. The Electronics & Communications Engineering department has set up advanced laboratory facilities and the students are trained in these laboratories regularly by allotting suitable time slots in the regular time table.
Students of both the departments have accesses to the high speed broad band internet connection and a well equipped department library. Students are encouraged to participate in national & International level conferences,workshops & present papers.

MBA

"Acquisition of knowledge leads to power; but pledge of our department is to transform knowledge beyond 'power' leading to VIRTUE"

The post Graduate department Of Management Studies (M.B.A) was established in 2007 to offer two year full time Master of Business Administration Program. Our M.B.A program approved by AICTE & is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum, Karnataka.

The Curriculum spread evenly over 4 semesters. The first & Second semester of the program is common for all students. Specialization offered in 3 & 4 semesters in any one of these electives: Marketing, Finance, Human resource Management, Information System, International Business or Banking & Insurance.

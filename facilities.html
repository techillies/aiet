DISTINCT FEATURES/ SUPPORT SERVICES

Library, laboratories, Seminar/Conference Halls and Class rooms are air conditioned as well as equipped with modern educational aids. Separate computer centre with internet connectivity for MBA students.

SOCIAL PROJECT & BUSINESS SEMINAR

In the social projects students will involve with the social issues pertain to business Environment. In business seminar students have to choose one topic of this/ his area of specialization & use the primary / second data for presentations.

SOFT SKILLS TRAINING

The department provides basic & advanced soft skills of English & personality development to the students to prepare for them for placements.

CONTINUOUS COUNSELING

The Department lays great emphasis on making students self reliant & value driven towards this end, a continuous feed back and counseling provided. From the academic year 2005-2006

AIR CONDITIONED CLASSROOMS

Ours is the first engineering institution in the entire state to provide air-conditioned class rooms. With this the entire college is air-conditioned. Govt of Karnataka declares half day working during summer in Gulbarga & neighboring districts. However his doesn’t help in completing the course effectively. In view of this our President Poojya Appaji provided air conditioned class rooms along with air conditioned labs, library, seminar halls etc. without increasing the fees.

VIDEO CONFERENCING FACILITY "TWO WAY BROAD BAND"

Ours is the only Institution in the entire Karnataka state to invest Rs 50 lacks for the installation of two way broad band video conferencing facility for an interactive digital class room at our institution under the AICTE scheme of inter linking of institution of higher education for resources sharing. Our students are going to get world class training and video access to the experts with a click of button though KU band of EDUSAT, India’s exclusive satellite education.

ATELLITE INTERACTIVE TERMINAL BY ISRO

Recently Indian Space Research Organization had installed satellite interactive terminal trough EDUSAT .This facility is available only in 30 engineering colleges in Karnataka .ISRO has chosen our college as one of the center and a studio had been created in our institution. This facility helps our students and teachers to interact with their counterparts for resource sharing.

SUN MICRO SYSTEM LAB

Ours is one of the very few engineering colleges in the entire Karnataka State to establish the Sun Knowledge lab in collaboration with SUN Microsystems USA.Our institution invested Rs.10 lakhs for the establishment of SUN knowledge lab at our institution to train our students in the area of JAVA technology as per the industry requirements on 10 number of Sun work stations installed by he SUN Microsystems. The software worth of RS.15 crores is supplied by the SUN Microsystems USA for training our students.

N. C. C AIR WING

Ours is the first institution in which along with the N.C.C Army, N.C.C Air wing is being started from 2005-06. N.C.C Air wing of Indian air force provides the right training to the technical graduates so that they can become highly discipline, efficient technical graduates, eligible to take up challenging carrier life that in Indian Air force. Such training facilities are available only in few places in entire country. An aircraft (two seater) is being purchased and runway is being built with an estimated cost of Rs.1 crores.

ORACLE LAB

We have tied up with Oracle India Ltd .through Board of IT Education standards (BITES), Govt of Karnataka for the establishment of Oracle lab at our institution. The oracle corporation provides software worth Rs. 5 Crores for training our students in Oracle technology.

Admissions Procedure for UG

Eligibility criteria for admission to I Semester B.E.

Academic Eligibility:

1).The Candidate has passed the II-Year Pre-University or XII Std. or equivalent exams with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry, Bio-Technology, Computer Science, Biology, Electronics & has secured not less than 45% of the aggregate marks in optional subjects with English as one of the languages for admission to Engineering and Technology courses.

2).A Candidate shall be eligible for admission to any of the full time degree courses is the holder of a diploma in appropriate branch of engineering awarded by the Board of Technical Education in Karnataka or equivalent qualification and has secured not less than 45% of marks in the final year Diploma examinations. Provided that in respect of Architecture the Candidate must have secured not less than 50% of the marks in the final year Diploma Examination and appeared for Aptitude test (NATA).

Selection Procedure:

1).Generally, admission is done on the basis of performance in the Entrance Exam (CET / COMED-K) for all the B.E. / B.Arch. courses

2).Regarding allotment of Management quota seats, please contact our Head Office Telephone No:08472-231192.

Admissions Procedure for PG

Academic Eligibility for M.E. / M.Tech / M.Arch courses

For M.E. / M. Tech / M. Arch courses:
Option-1: Candidates with valid GATE score.
Option-2: PGCET-2014 Candidates
(i) For Full Time M.E. / M. Tech / M. Arch courses: A candidate who has passed prescribed qualifying examination or equivalent examination and obtained an aggregate minimum of 50% marks taken together in all the subjects in all the years is eligible for admission to M.E. / M. Tech / M. Arch courses. 45% of marks in case of SC, ST and Category-I candidates.
(ii) For Part Time M.E. / M. Tech / M. Arch courses: A candidate who has passed prescribed qualifying examination or equivalent examination and obtained an aggregate minimum of 50% marks taken together in all the subjects in all the years is eligible for admission to M.E. / M. Tech / M. Arch / courses. 45% of marks in case of SC, ST and Category-I candidates.
In addition to this candidate after obtaining the complete degree, must have worked for not less than three years before the issue of notification for the relevant year, in an establishment and must be working in an organization situated within a radial distance of 40 km from the institution in which the candidate seeks admission. Based on the performance of the candidates in the Common Entrance Test, the rank list will be prepared and published for admission to the above courses. Therefore the candidates are advised to attend the counselling only if they are having the requisite percentage of marks in the qualifying examination. (i.e., should have obtained 50% of aggregate marks of all the years of the Degree examination. 45% of aggregate marks of all the years in the Degree examination in case of candidates belonging to SC , ST and Category-I)

NOTE
1. Candidates who do not pass in all the subjects in the qualifying examination and candidates who do not possess the academic eligibility detailed above for various courses, will not be eligible for selection of seats in the respective courses.
2. The SC / ST / Category-I / OBC eligibility criteria are applicable to persons of Karnataka origin who are claiming eligibility for Government seats under clauses (a) and (b) and the same is not applicable to non-Karnataka students.

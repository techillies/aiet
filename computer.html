Computer Science And Engineerning

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 2002 with an intake of 60 students and in 2010 it is extended 120 students and has grown over the years in terms of stature and infrastructure. It is well equipped to impart the latest knowledge demanded by the industries. The department has all the latest software and hardware to keep the students abreast with the emerging technologies. All computers are with latest configuration and with uninterruptible power back up. Institute-Industry interaction is maintained by simulating software industry work environment, arranging study tours, undertaking sponsored projects, arranging seminars by experts from industries on recent topics.Department constantly monitors the academic performance of students, identifying weaker students and counseling them for their improvements. The department also conducts co-curricular courses on latest software and programming languages as per industry demands. The department conducts periodic Tech-Fest, FDP, Workshops, Seminars, symposiums and conferences to help the faculties, students and research scholars. The department has very good placement record.

Recent Activities
Two days workshop on Network Simulator-2 sponsored by ISTE, Bangalore.
Four days Faculty development programme on Oracle PL/SQL sponsored by VTU-VGST of amount 2,00,000 rupees.
Prof. Basavaraj B. Mathapati, Head of the Department, Computer Science & Engineering

Dr.V.D Mytri

EXPERIENCE

He has to his credit a of Total Teaching and R & D Experience of over 38 years.

PROFESSIONAL QUALIFICATION

Completed his B.E. (E&C E in the year 1972, form Karnataka University, Dharwad. and did his M.Tech.(Communication Engg.) in the year 1980 from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. After completing his post graduation he was awarded Ph.D. (Digital Communication) IN THE YEAR 1988 by Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. POSITION HELD: He started his carrier as a Lecturer, PDA College of Engg. Gulbarga, in the year 1974 and was selected as Assistant Professor, PDACE, Gulbarga in the month of April, 1984 and he worked in the said capacity till January, 1994. He became Professor, PDACE, Gulbarga in Feb. 1994 and he was in the said capacity till January, 2002. He has been Principal, PDA College of Engg. Gulbarga from Feb. 2002 to Dec. 2007, Principal, Guru Nanak Dev Engg. College, Bidar from Feb. 2008 to April 2012 and Principal, Shetty Institute of Technology Gulbarga April 2012 to till date. He has held the various positions in the field of academics: as Dean, Chairman, Board of Studies, Chairman, Board of Studies for Post Graduate Engineering, Chairman board of Examinations in Gulbarga university till 1998 and also VTU Belgaum. He has also held the academically coveted positions in VTU as Executive Council and Academic Senate Member.

COUNTRIES VISITED

He is a widely travelled person and some of the countries visited for his academic pursuit are Germany, United States of America and Mauritius.

PROFESSIONAL/TECHNICAL PAPER PUBLICATIONS

He has more than 25 papers to his credit and presently guiding five candidates for Ph.D. He has also interaction with professors abroad for R&D Work.

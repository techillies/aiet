About Library

Our library is fully Air Conditioned and is kept open round the clock which is special features of our Institution for the first time in the entire Karnataka State .A new Library hall with 700 Sq.mt. area with a seating capacity of 250 students is created. The library has subscription to about 100 numbers of national international journals and magazines. A total of 25.000 books both reference and text books are made available. We are the first institute in Karnataka to provide Science & Technology encyclopedia worth Rs.2.5 lakhs from USA.The library is also provided with internet facility and is being converted into fully digital with an estimated cost of Rs.50 lacs.
Our library became the member of INDEST-AICTE consortium through which an e-library facility is established with an investment of Rs.10 lakhs. Through E-library our student get connected to world's reputed publications like IEEE and IEE and access more than 125 journals, hundreds of new reference books, more than 25000, pages new technical materials every month, Delnet facility is also available in our library.

Prayaas

Prayaas is the first technical festival of one of its kind in the Gulbarga Region. The brain child of V.O.I.C.E, a forum of Information and Computer Science, it aims at mining out the talent of the young technocrats.

"Only the first step is always difficult after that you can even cross the seas of the world with firm will."

Prayaas 2011 was our first endeavour. With the overall effort it has out-spoken with its novel competative events, benevolent hospitality and not to miss out the tremendous fun.

Civil Engineering

Profession of civil engineering retains it ancient aura even in the current era of electronics and computer revolution. This branch of engineering touches to entire need of society, civilians that includes shelter and water, roads and bridges, dam and tunnels, environmental pollution and similar construction activities directly related to welfare of human beings. As long as civilization extracts and grows civil engineering profession will persist.
Established in 2012, the Department of Civil Engineering with an intake of 60 students and in 2013 it is extended 120 students and has grown over the years in terms of stature and infrastructure.The department is having highly qualified faculties. The department has well-established laboratories of modern equipment in the surveying, Material Testing, concrete technology, fluid mechanics, geology etc.

Prof. Rajashekar Yargol, Head of the Department, Civil Engineering.
